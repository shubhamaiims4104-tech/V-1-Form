About the Project

This research project studies how digital devices, mobile phones, and games affect children’s attention span, emotions, and learning habits.

Goal

  • Understand phone and gaming addiction in children
  • Collect research data from teachers, parents, and students
  • Promote healthy digital habits
  • Encourage outdoor and creative activities
